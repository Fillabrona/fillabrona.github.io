About the Game

Quiz for Survival is an intense trivia experience that challenges your knowledge, strategy, and risk management with over 2,500 questions. Correct answers award points and coins, while wrong answers drain your health. Use the shop to buy items like Health Potions, Mystery Boxes, and Lucky Triples. With multiple modes like Gauntlet and Shopless, and 5 difficulty levels, can you survive?
